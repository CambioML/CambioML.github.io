Welcome to uniflow!
===================================

**uniflow** is an open-source python library for ML scientists and practitioners.
uniflow helps you quickly prepare LLM finetuning data, from your private and unstructured data including PDFs, HTMLs, PPTs, Images, etc. With uniflow, you can easily extract and chunk text, generate questions and answers, summarize text, etc. for preparing your private LLMs finetuning.
